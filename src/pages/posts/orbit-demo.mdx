<div class="sim-article">
  <h2 id="about-this-page">About this page</h2>
  <p>
    Above is a <strong>real-time orbiting particles visualization</strong> built with Three.js.
    Thousands of instanced spheres drift on circular paths with subtle vertical flutter. Use your mouse or trackpad to rotate and zoom.
  </p>

  <h3 id="what-youre-seeing">What you’re seeing</h3>
  <p>
    Each particle follows a slightly different radius, speed, and phase. Colors vary with distance from the center to give depth.
    Camera motion uses <code>OrbitControls</code> with damping for smooth interaction.
  </p>

  <h2 id="tech-notes">Tech notes</h2>
  <p>
    This demo renders an <strong>InstancedMesh</strong> of small spheres (≈1k) with a single draw call.
    Window resizing is handled via <code>ResizeObserver</code>, and the component exposes a small
    <code>start/stop</code> API that’s wired to the Play/Pause button above.
  </p>
</div>
